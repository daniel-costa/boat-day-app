Your Active BoatDay

Since you are on an ongoing BoatDay, you can access directly all important features that help you to enjoy a safe trip in this screen.

You can chat with other guests to coordinate, call your host in case you might be delayed or open the map to find your BoatDay easily.

If you want to capture awesome moments from your BoatDay, just tap on "Take Pictures". The pictures will be automatically uploaded to your BoatDay and you can take a look in Past BoatDays. In case of an emergency, do not hesitate to use the emergency service hotlines of Boat USA or the Coast Guard to ensure everyone's safety.
